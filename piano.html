Piano Visualizer

This file is meant to explore the possibility of automating the creation of videos to visualize the keys pressed in songs I play.

I wrote this for myself to visualize the keystrokes I use when playing my music. I have been teaching myself piano over the past two years, though I cannot read musical notation...

Project Goals

  • Visualizer: Templates and customizations
  • Combining sound and video: Automating sync process

This is a proof of concept stage, more to come!

Current Progress

Currently, I am working on a project to visualize drum tracks. I am exploring what it takes to process the videos assuming the MIDI tracks contain multiple files.

This is intended to be a way to improve my playing but they are fun to make/watch too!

These videos are the first two examples I processed that had the additional tracks to manage. Note: I need a system to organize the files I produce. In addition, piano lessons are probably wise.
